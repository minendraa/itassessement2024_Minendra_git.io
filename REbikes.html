Some interesting facts about Royal Enfield.





These are some bikes that are available in Nepali Market at the time.



Hunter 350


    The Royal Enfield Hunter 350 is a stylish and modern motorcycle designed for urban commuting and weekend rides.
    With a classic yet contemporary look, it offers a comfortable riding position and a smooth, responsive engine.
    The Hunter 350 is equipped with modern features such as LED lighting, digital instrument cluster, and ABS for safety. It is powered by a 350cc single-cylinder engine, delivering a balance of power and efficiency.
    Whether cruising through city streets or exploring the open road, the Royal Enfield Hunter 350 offers a thrilling riding experience.
    Price in Nepal: Rs. 4,75,000

Classic Reborn 350


    The Royal Enfield Reborn Classic 350 is a timeless and iconic motorcycle that blends vintage styling with modern technology.
    With its classic design elements such as the thumping exhaust note, hand-painted fuel tank, and retro-inspired instrumentation, it pays homage to Royal Enfield's heritage.
    The Reborn Classic 350 is powered by a reliable and torquey 350cc engine, delivering a smooth and comfortable ride.
    It features modern upgrades such as electric start, disc brakes, and fuel injection for improved performance and convenience.
    Whether cruising through the countryside or navigating city streets, the Royal Enfield Reborn Classic 350 offers a nostalgic riding experience with a touch of modernity.

    Price in Nepal: Rs. 5,75,000

Scram 411


    The Royal Enfield Scram 411 is a rugged and versatile motorcycle designed for adventure and exploration.
    With its scrambler-style design, high-mounted exhaust, and off-road-friendly features, it is ready to tackle a variety of terrain.
    The Scram 411 is powered by a 411cc single-cylinder engine, providing ample power and torque for off-road adventures.
    It features a comfortable riding position, long-travel suspension, and dual-purpose tires for enhanced off-road performance.
    Conquering dirt trails or navigating city streets, the Royal Enfield Scram 411 offers a thrilling and capable riding experience for adventure enthusiasts.

    Price in Nepal: Rs. 6,60,000

Himalayan


    The Royal Enfield Himalayan 450 is a rugged adventure touring motorcycle designed to conquer the toughest terrains.
    With its go-anywhere attitude, robust chassis, and long-travel suspension, it is built to handle both on-road and off-road adventures with ease.
    The Himalayan 450 is powered by a 450cc single-cylinder engine, providing ample power and torque for long-distance rides.
    It features a comfortable upright riding position, windscreen, and large fuel tank for extended touring capabilities.
    Whether exploring mountain trails or cruising on the highway, the Royal Enfield Himalayan 450 offers a reliable and versatile adventure touring experience.

    Price in Nepal(expected): Rs. 9,99,999
