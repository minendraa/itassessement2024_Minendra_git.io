Some interesting facts about Jawa:

    Historic Legacy: Jawa is a Czech motorcycle brand with a rich history dating back to 1929 when it was founded in Prague. The brand has a strong racing heritage and was known for producing reliable and innovative motorcycles.
    Two-stroke Engines: Jawa motorcycles were known for their distinctive two-stroke engine designs. These engines were popular for their simplicity, lightweight, and good power output, making them ideal for racing and everyday commuting.
    Global Presence: Jawa motorcycles gained popularity not only in Europe but also in various parts of Asia, Africa, and South America. The brand's motorcycles were known for their durability and performance, making them a popular choice for riders around the world.
    Revival: After ceasing production in the 1990s, the Jawa brand was revived in 2018 by Classic Legends Pvt. Ltd., a subsidiary of Mahindra & Mahindra, with new models inspired by the classic Jawa designs. The revival has been well-received by enthusiasts, maintaining the brand's legacy while incorporating modern technology.
    Innovation in Technology: Jawa was one of the first motorcycle manufacturers to introduce features like a four-speed gearbox, telescopic front forks, and hydraulic dampers, which were considered advanced for their time and contributed to the brand's reputation for innovation.

Jawa Perak


    Jawa Perak is a bobberstyle motorcycle from the iconic Czech brand Jawa.
    It is known for its sleek and minimalist design, characterized by a low-slung stance and a single-seat setup.
    The Perak is powered by a 334cc, liquid-cooled, single-cylinder engine, delivering a smooth and refined performance. It features a blacked-out theme with minimal chrome accents, giving it a bold and aggressive look.
    The motorcycle comes with modern features such as ABS, a digital instrument cluster, and LED taillights.
